Overweight terms & conditions
No overweight container shall exceed 20,000 lbs per axle, and no more than 12,000 lbs on the steer axles. The shipper and/or consignee is responsible for ensuring cargo weight is within permitted limits prior to dispatch. Any and all fines, penalties, or citations incurred as a result of exceeding permit or legal weight limits will be charged back in full to the responsible party. This includes but is not limited to: all associated towing fees, rework, re-stuffing, reweigh fees, storage, and any additional transportation costs required to bring the shipment into compliance. By tendering this load, the shipper agrees to these terms.
